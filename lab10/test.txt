More on Data Studio. Google launched Data Studio in March 2016 as part of the enterprise Google Analytics 360 suite,
 with a free version that lets you turn your data into informative, easy-to-read, easy-to-share,
 and fully customizable dashboards and reports. There are easy-to-use drag-and-drop features to customize reports
 from various data sources, including Search Console,
Google Analytics, Google Ads, and much more.
